## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, other developers, and design enthusiasts who visit Harry Fan's personal digital home to view his portfolio and read his blog.

### Brand Personality
Bold, minimal, refined, editorial. The brand speaks with confidence and avoids generic tech-bro tropes. It embraces high contrast and distinctive typography over safe choices.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Neo-Brutalist / Editorial Minimal**. 
- Monochromatic foundation (off-white/off-black) with stark lines.
- Huge, expressive typography (`Bricolage Grotesque`) mixed with clean body text (`Space Grotesk`).
- Rejection of "AI slop" (no soft gradients, no generic drop shadows, no nested rounded cards).
- Intentional asymmetry and generous spacing.
- Motion is purposeful and uses exponential easing.

### Design Principles
1. **Typography as Interface**: Let the words be the primary visual element. Use massive type scales.
2. **Raw over Refined**: Prefer stark lines and high contrast over soft shadows and blurs.
3. **Intentional Rhythm**: Use varied spacing and break the grid for emphasis.
4. **No AI Slop**: Avoid gradients, rounded cards with emojis, and standard 3-column feature grids.
